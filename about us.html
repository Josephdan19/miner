About Us


Bitblock-Miner is a cryptocurrency cloud-mining system where uses can buy mining power using Bitcoin or Ethereum to become passive miners, and earn Bitcoin and Ethereum as per their staking amount



Bitblock-Miner is a cloud mining platform created by specialists which is one of the biggest providers of computational power in mining Bitcoin and Ethereum cryptocurrency with mining network laboratories in the Austtralia, with our services accessible through the world through the internet. Through tremendous work made by the engineers, developer and other specialists of the company as well as partner companies, we are working towards achieving the best online cloud Mining platform with highest transperency rate.
The team of Bitblock-Miner has always put the task of making Bitcoin and Ethereum mining accessible not for only crypto enthusiasts but also common users.
